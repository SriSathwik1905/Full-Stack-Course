About The Modern Frame

Our History

Founded in 2010, The Modern Frame has become a hub for contemporary artists and art lovers. Our mission is to showcase innovative works and foster creative dialogue.

Located in the heart of the arts district, our gallery spans two floors of carefully curated exhibition space. We pride ourselves on discovering and nurturing emerging talent while also showcasing established artists who continue to push boundaries.
